# Pokie Palace — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 11 July 2026_

Pokie Palace ("the app", "we") is a social casino game published by Aden
Kingi, based in Tauranga, New Zealand. It is for entertainment only:
virtual coins have no real-world value and cannot be bought with, or
exchanged for, real money or prizes.

> A browser-renderable version of this policy for the Play Console privacy
> URL lives at `docs/privacy-policy.html`.

## Information you provide to us

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no contact forms. We do not
collect your name, email, phone number, or similar personal details.

## Information stored on your device

Game progress is stored locally on your device only: coin balance, bet
size, level and XP, unlocked machines, piggy-bank total, daily/hourly claim
timestamps, sound settings, and a local-only gameplay log (spin counts and
win/loss totals) used for balancing. This never leaves your device and is
deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app shows ads via **Google AdMob** — optional rewarded ads for extra
coins, and occasional interstitials between machine switches. To serve and
measure ads, Google's advertising SDK may collect and process device
information including the advertising identifier, IP address, coarse
IP-based location, and app-interaction/device data. This is done by Google
as an independent party under [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
We do not receive or store this data; we see only aggregate, non-identifying
ad-performance totals. See the `.html` version for the full disclosure and
opt-out instructions.

## Children

The app simulates gambling and is intended for a mature audience (17+). It
is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from them.
