Pokie Palace ("the app") is a social casino game for entertainment only.
Virtual coins have no real-world value and cannot be cashed out.

## What we collect

Nothing. The app runs fully offline and does not transmit any personal
data to us or to third parties.

## What stays on your device

Game state is stored locally on your device only (via your browser's or
device's local storage): coin balance, bet size, level and XP, unlocked
machines, piggy-bank total, daily/hourly claim timestamps, sound settings,
and a local-only gameplay event log used for balancing (spin counts and
win/loss totals). This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app
(or clearing site data) deletes it permanently.

## Children

The app contains simulated gambling and is intended for adult players. It
does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
